Hyderabadi Biriyani

Let's Start

We should make Biryani as per the ratio of Chicken.

Now, We are using 1kg of Chicken.

Make the Fried Onion :

IngrdientsQuantity
Onion100 gm
Refined Oil or Olive Oil50 ml

Procedure :

  1. switch on the stove and place the Pan.
  2. Put the 50 ml of Oil and 100 gm of Onion.
  3. Saute well until the Onion Looks Golden Brown.
  4. Switch of your stove.
  5. Take two bowls.
  6. Take half of the Fried Onion without Oil and Put in Bowl.
  7. Take the another bowl and put the Remaining Fried Onion with Oil.
  • Now, Fried Onion is Ready!

Marinate the Chicken :

IngrdientsQuantity
Chicken1 kg
Saltto the taste
Lemon Juice1 tbsp
Ginger Garlic Paste2 tbsp
Coriander Powder2 tbsp
Chilli Powder1 tbsp
Gram Masala1 tbsp
Sliced Chilli3 nos
Mint Leaf50 gm
Coriander Leaf50 gm
Cinnamon Stick3 nos
Green Cardamon3 nos
Cloves5 nos
Star Anise1 nos
Bay Leaf1 nos
Jeera1/2 tbsp
Pepper1/2 tbsp
Chilli Powder1 tbsp
Refined Oil or Olive Oil50 ml
Fried Onion50 gm
Cashew Nutsto the taste

Procedure :

  1. Take all the Ingrdients as per the Ratio.
  2. Take a big bowl.
  3. Wash the Chicken with 1/2 tbsp of Turmeric.
  4. Then, Put the Chicken in the Bowl.
  5. Add all the Ingridents in the Bowl as per the Ratio.
  6. Mix the Ingridents & Chicken well. Till the Aroma comes.
  7. Then, Close the bowl and Let it Marinate for mimimum period of 2hrs.
  • Now, Marinated Chicken is Ready!

Prepare the Rice :

IngrdientsQuantity
Basmati Rice1 kg
Cloves5 nos
Relined Oil or Olive Oil2tbsp
Green Cardamon3 nos
Salt1 tbsp
Water1.5 litre

procedure :

  1. Wash the Basmati Rice.
  2. Switch on the Stove
  3. Pour the Water in the Dabara.
  4. Wait till the water reaches the boiling point.
  5. then put the Salt and Stir!.
  6. Put the Washed Basmati Rice.and
  7. put the 2 tbsp of Refined Oil or Olive Oil and Stir ones!.
  8. Boil the Rice to 70% of Consistency and the strain the rice from boil water.
  9. Keep the Rice boiled Water for later use.
  • Now, Flavoured Basmati Rice is Ready!

Make the Hyderbadi Biriyani :

IngrdientsQuantity
Gheeto the taste
Cloves3 nos
Green Cardamon3 nos
Coriander Leaf50gm
Mint Leaf5ogm
Cashew Nutsto the taste
Dried Graphesto the taste
Saffron1/4 tbsp

Procedure :

  1. Switch on the Stove.
  2. Keep the flame in sim.
  3. Put the Marinated Chickenand add 1/2 glass of water.
  4. wait till the Chicken cook well and
  5. Then add the half of the Flavoured Basmati Rice and spread evenly in the Dabara.
  6. Then add the Garnishing Items(Ghee, Green Cardamon, Coriander Leaf, Mint Leaf, Cashew Nuts, Dried Graphes, Fried Onion) in half of the Quanity.
  7. Add the Remaining Flavoured Basmati Rice and Spread evenly.
  8. Add the Remaining Garnishing Items and
  9. Pour the Saffron evenly.
  10. leave the flame in sim and check every 5mins until the Rice Cook well.
  • Bravo! We Made it!
  • Tasty Hyderabadi Biriyani is Ready!
